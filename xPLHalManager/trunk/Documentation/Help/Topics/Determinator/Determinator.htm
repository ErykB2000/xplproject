Determinators

xPLHal is renowned for it's capabilities as a flexible and powerful scripting engine, based on the VBScript language. Until recently, using xPLHal to control your home would have required a degree of VBScript to be written.

The introduction of Determinators removes the need for users to write scripts, allowing powerful control of xPL devices through a series of wizards and easy-to-use graphical interfaces.

This section will tell you everything you need to know about Determinators, ranging from step-by-step guides, to explanations of advanced features.
